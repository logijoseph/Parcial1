Tribute to Steve Jobs

"The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do."

TIMELINE

  • Feb 24, 1995 Steven Paul Jobs is born in San Francisco to Joanne Carole Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali. The then-unnmarried coupe give up their son to adoption. Paul and Clara Jobs become Jobs' non-biological parents.
  • 1961 The Jobs family moves to Mountain View, Calif., part of what would later become known as Silicon Valley.
  • August 9, 2011 Apple shares edge past those of Exxon Mobil to become the most valuable U.S. company, in terms of market capitalization, at $337.17 bilion.
  • August 24, 2011 Resigns from Apple CEO post; becomes chariman.
